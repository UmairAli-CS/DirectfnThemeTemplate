DFN RA CLIENT profiling

DFN ra portfolio assignment

  • profile-PORTFOLIO matching

An algorithm matches one of the client's predefined investment portfolios with the investor's determined risk profile.

  • automated + semi-automated

As the user portfolio is created the DFN-RA invests in a predefined securities universe through a full or a semi automatized process.
